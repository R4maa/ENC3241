# Instructions

# How to Pump Gas

**Introduction:** This guide explains how to fill a car tank with gas.

1. Locate the side of the car with fuel door (as indicated by arrow on fuel gauge)
    <img src="https://scontent-mia3-3.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.30808-6/484966139_1065533265603535_163533467610309510_n.jpg?_nc_cat=110&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=7b2446&_nc_ohc=XrSOVcPLUN8Q7kNvwEwHvD0&_nc_oc=AdoHOBC9CCPgXqS8hYBv2Uv5fBn8VxBR1PVXMwcM8bVGOIPbvgi8sLZ2tpUOCDAlSQE&_nc_zt=23&_nc_ht=scontent-mia3-3.xx&_nc_gid=B16Ylv51SI3wRs4gJHXqfQ&_nc_ss=7a3a8&oh=00_Af21oKgdMiUdusFMspklN0SQ66l1mIy7mLhxqX9AF5fsVA&oe=69EE4A2F" alt="Fuel gauge indicating the fuel door location">
   *(Figure 1. A fuel gauge with an arrow indicating the fuel door location on left side. Source: Al Asiri Motors Facebook Page)*
2. Park next to gas pump with car's fuel door near the gas pump
3. Turn the vehicle off completely
4. Insert payment card into pump
5. Follow directions prompted by screen (varies by gas station)
6. Open fuel door by pressing in on the car or using interior door release lever
    <img src="https://help.getgo.sg/hc/article_attachments/41131081880601" alt="Fuel door lever located below steering wheel">
   *(Figure 2. The fuel door lever located below the steering wheel on a Mazda CX-5. Source: GetGo Help Centre)*
7. Remove gas cap inside fuel door by twisting counterclockwise
8. Place removed gas cap on interior of fuel door
9. Insert gas nozzle into tank
    <img src="https://di-uploads-pod23.dealerinspire.com/bobtylertoyota/uploads/2024/05/iStock-153794211.jpg" alt="Person inserting gas pump nozzle">
   *(Figure 3. A person inserting gas pump nozzle into fuel tank. Source: Bob Tyler Toyota)*
10. Select desired fuel grade
11. Squeeze handle until desired fuel is reached
12. Return fuel nozzle to pump
13. Screw gas cap clockwise on until you hear a click
14. Close fuel door
15. Return to car
